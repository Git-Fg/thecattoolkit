## Template 4: Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

```markdown
# Q4 2024 Business Review

## Executive Summary
- Revenue: $4.2M (↑ 23% QoQ)
- Customers: 847 (↑ 18% QoQ)
- Churn: 3.2% (↓ 0.8% QoQ)
- NPS: 68 (↑ 5 points)

## Quarter Highlights
**Major Win:** Enterprise tier launched
**Growth:** 3 largest deals closed
**Launch:** Self-serve product catalog
**Team:** Hired VP of Sales

## Performance vs Goals

| Metric         | Target | Actual | Variance |
|----------------|--------|--------|----------|
| Revenue        | $4.0M  | $4.2M  | +5%      |
| New Customers  | 175    | 162    | -7%      |
| Expansion      | $300K  | $425K  | +42%     |
| Churn          | <4%    | 3.2%   | +20%     |

## Customer Insights

**High Performers (Top 20%):**
- Average contract: $52K
- Implementation: 14 days
- Expansion: 180% within 12 months

**At-Risk Indicators:**
- No login for 7+ days
- Support ticket volume increasing
- Feature adoption < 30% at day 30

## Competitive Landscape
- Competitor A: Launched similar feature (Q3)
- Competitor B: Raised $50M Series C
- Our advantage: Superior integration ecosystem

## Investment Areas
1. **Sales Ops** (Q1 2025)
   - CRM optimization
   - Sales enablement content
   - Target: $5.2M revenue

2. **Product Dev** (Q1-Q2 2025)
   - API v2 launch
   - Mobile app
   - Target: 40% faster implementation

3. **Marketing** (Q1 2025)
   - Content engine
   - Partner program
   - Target: 30% self-serve pipeline

## Risk Factors
- Hiring: 2 key positions still open
- Technical debt in billing system
- Enterprise sales cycle lengthening

## Ask
- Approve $800K for Q1 hiring
- Prioritize billing system refactor
- Decision needed: Partner strategy (Jan 15)
```
